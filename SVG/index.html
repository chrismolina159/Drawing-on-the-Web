Learn About Video Game Icons!

Icons in Video Games are a universal language. For most games a bag with a dollar sign or a coin means money, and for some reason many health potions are filled with a red liquid. Here are some other common icons in video games.

The crossed sword symbol is used a lot in roleplaying games, action gamesm and some strategy games. They can be used for signs to represent a shop where one can purchase weapons, or sometimes this symbol is next to a number to represent how much damage a character does.

The ring symbol is commonly used to represent accesories that a character can equip. It's fitting seeing as to how a ring is an accesory for people in real life.

The wand/staff is commonly used to represent magic of some sort. Whether it's describing someone's magical strength, a class, an item, etc, anything magic related will likely see some staff next to it.

A key can be an actual key that a player picks up, or sometimes a key is used to represent a key item that a player needs to further themselves along in a game.

The health icon stretches into all types of video games. It's used to represent a player's health or maybe this icon is on the center of a med-kit of some kind to show players that this item restores your health. The health icon is most likely the most used icon in video games.
